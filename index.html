Delete User Data

This page provides instructions on how to delete user data for the game Millionaire using Google Play Games service.

Note: We do not create any internal account for the game. Instead, we utilize the "Google Play Games" service to manage scores and rating lists. To remove data from the "Play Games", please follow the instructions below:

Instructions:

  1. Go to your games profile page https://play.google.com/games/profile.
  2. Click 'Your data'.
  3. Select 'Delete Play Games account & data'.
  4. Find the app "Millionaire" and click 'Delete'.
  5. Follow the prompts to confirm and accept the deletion.
